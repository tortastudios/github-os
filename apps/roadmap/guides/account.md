# Create a GitHub Account

Your GitHub account is your identity across everything you'll build. It's where your code, projects, and professional history live. Pick a username you'd put on a business card -- you'll use it for a long time.

## How to do it

1. Go to [github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Choose a username (short, professional, easy to remember)
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link GitHub sends you

## Try it

```
Go to github.com/signup and create your account. If you already have one, sign in and review your profile at github.com/settings/profile.
```

## Resources

- [GitHub signup](https://github.com/signup)
- [Setting up your profile](https://docs.github.com/en/account-and-profile/setting-up-and-managing-your-github-profile/customizing-your-profile/about-your-profile)

## Tips

- Your username appears in every URL for your projects (github.com/username/project), so keep it clean and simple.
- You can change your username later, but it breaks existing links. Choose carefully up front.
