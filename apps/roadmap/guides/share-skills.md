# Share Your Skills and Commands

Skills and commands are just files. Share them by linking to your repo, or create a separate template repo of your best skills. Others can copy them into their own OS. This is how the ecosystem grows.

## How to do it

1. Make sure your repo is **public** (Settings > General > Danger Zone > Change visibility)
2. Share the direct link to a skill or command file
3. Others can copy files from your .claude/skills/ and .claude/commands/ into their own repos
4. Or mark your repo as a **template** (Settings > General > Template repository) so anyone can clone the whole thing

## Try it

```
Pick your best custom command or skill. Share the GitHub link with someone who has their own OS repo and help them add it.
```

## Resources

- [Creating a template repository](https://docs.github.com/en/repositories/creating-and-managing-repositories/creating-a-template-repository)

## Tips

- Template repos are the easiest way to share a full setup. The other person gets everything without affecting your original.
- When sharing individual skills, include a brief explanation of what it does and how to use it.
