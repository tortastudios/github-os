# Write Your First Journal Entry

The journal/ folder is your thinking space. Write daily notes, weekly reflections, ideas, lessons learned. There are no rules -- it's for you. The value builds over time: searchable, versioned, always accessible. Your AI tools can also read it for context.

## How to do it

1. Open the journal/ folder in your repo
2. Create a new file (like week-1.md or today's date)
3. Write whatever's on your mind -- what you're working on, what you learned, what's next
4. Commit it
5. Repeat as often as you like

## Try it

```
Create a file called journal/week-1.md. Write 3 sentences about what you're working on this week and what you hope to accomplish.
```

## Tips

- Don't overthink format. A few sentences is fine. Consistency matters more than length.
- Date your entries so you can look back and see how your thinking evolved.
- Your AI tools can read your journal for context, which makes their advice more relevant over time.
