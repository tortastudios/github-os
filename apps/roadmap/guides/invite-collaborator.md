# Invite a Collaborator

When you're ready to go multiplayer, invite someone to your repo. They can create issues, submit PRs, and use the same skills and commands. This is how a personal OS becomes a team OS.

## How to do it

1. Go to your repo's **Settings** tab
2. Click **Collaborators** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Add people**
4. Search for their GitHub username or email
5. They'll get an invitation email and need to accept
6. Once accepted, they can push, create issues, and open PRs

## Try it

```
Go to Settings > Collaborators and invite someone you work with. Create an issue and assign it to them.
```

## Tips

- Collaborators get full access by default. Use branch protection rules if you want to require PR reviews before merging.
- Share your agents.md with collaborators so their AI tools behave the same way yours does.
