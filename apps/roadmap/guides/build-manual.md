# Build Your Operating Manual

Your operating manual is the single most valuable file in your OS. It tells every AI tool what your business does, how you work, and where you need help. Once it exists, every future AI interaction has real context instead of starting from scratch.

## How to do it

1. Open your repo in any AI tool
2. Run `/build-manual` (or say "help me build my operating manual")
3. Answer the 6 questions about your business
4. Review the generated doc at `docs/operating-manual.md`
5. Edit anything that doesn't sound right
6. Commit with a descriptive message

## Try it

```
Run /build-manual and answer the questions. When it's done, test it by asking:
"Based on my operating manual, what should I focus on this week?"
```

## Tips

- Be specific in your answers. "I help small businesses" is less useful than "I help independent coffee shops set up online ordering."
- Update it when things change. It's a living document, not a one-time setup.
- The "Known Pain Points" section is especially valuable for AI -- it gives tools something concrete to help with.
